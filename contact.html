Frequently Asked Questions

Where exactly is the clinic located?

We're located at 19, Delhi Road, near MDU Campus, Delhi Bypass, Rohtak, Haryana 124021. It's just 15 minutes away from the main city center and easily accessible by all modes of transportation.

What are your clinic hours?

Monday to Saturday: 9:00 AM – 7:30 PM. We're closed on Sundays. We're open on public holidays with limited staff, so please call ahead.

Do you offer same-day emergency appointments?

Yes, we prioritize emergency cases. If you have a dental emergency, call us immediately at 095406 70276. We'll fit you in as soon as possible.

Is Dr. Vikramjet available every day?

Yes, Dr. Vikramjet Singh is our lead consultant and is available every day during clinic hours (except Sundays). We recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure his availability.

How much does a dental implant cost?

Dental implant costs vary depending on the type, material, and complexity of your case. We offer transparent pricing and a free consultation to discuss your specific needs and provide an accurate quote.

Is root canal treatment painful?

No, not with modern techniques. We use advanced rotary instruments and effective anesthesia. The procedure is virtually painless—comparable to a regular filling. Most patients feel relief afterward.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods: cash, UPI, credit cards, and debit cards. Choose the payment method that's most convenient for you.
