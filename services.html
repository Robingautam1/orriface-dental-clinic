Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions, Answered

Is root canal treatment painful?

+

No, root canal treatment is not painful. Modern rotary techniques and advanced anesthetics used at Oriiface make the procedure comfortable. In fact, root canal treatment relieves the pain caused by infection or inflammation. Most patients find it comparable to having a filling done.

How long do dental implants last?

+

Dental implants are designed to last 20-30 years or even a lifetime with proper care. Success depends on good oral hygiene, regular dental check-ups, and avoiding habits like smoking. Implants have a high success rate of over 95% when placed by experienced surgeons like Dr. Vikramjet.

What exactly is smile design?

+

Smile design is a comprehensive approach to enhancing your smile through various cosmetic procedures including teeth whitening, veneers, bonding, and gum contouring. We analyze your facial features, tooth shape, and color to create a customized plan that brings out your best smile.

Do you treat children at your clinic?

+

Yes, we have specialized pediatric dentistry services. We create a child-friendly environment with trained staff who make dental visits enjoyable. We focus on preventive care, education, and building positive relationships with dental health from an early age.

What's the cost range for braces and orthodontic treatment?

+

Orthodontic costs vary based on the complexity of the case and the type of braces chosen (metal, ceramic, or clear aligners). We offer transparent pricing and multiple payment options. During your consultation, we'll provide a detailed cost estimate and discuss financing options to make treatment affordable.

What are your clinic hours and can I book online?

+

We're open Monday to Saturday, 9AM to 7:30PM. You can reach us by phone at 095406 70276 or WhatsApp for appointments. We prioritize quick scheduling and can often accommodate same-day appointments for urgent cases.
