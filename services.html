Why Implants

The Gold Standard in Tooth Replacement

A dental implant is a titanium post surgically placed into the jawbone — acting as an artificial tooth root. Once integrated, a natural-looking crown is placed on top, giving you a permanent, stable tooth that you care for just like your own.

Lasts 20+ years with proper care
Prevents bone loss after tooth loss
No impact on neighbouring teeth
Indistinguishable from natural teeth

Root Canal Therapy

Save Your Tooth, End the Pain

Many patients fear root canals — but with modern rotary instruments and local anaesthesia, the procedure is as comfortable as getting a filling. We remove the infected pulp, clean the canals, and seal the tooth to relieve pain and preserve it for years to come.

Completed in a single appointment
Minimal discomfort with modern technique
Saves tooth from extraction
High success rate (95%+)
