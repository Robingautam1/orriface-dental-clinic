Who We Are

Redefining Dental Excellence in Rohtak

Oriiface Dental Care and Maxillofacial Center was established with a singular vision: to bring world-class dental care to the heart of Rohtak. Located on the Delhi Road near MDU campus, our clinic stands as a beacon of modern dentistry, combining advanced technology with personalized patient care.

We believe that every smile tells a story, and we're honored to be part of yours. Our mission is not just to treat teeth, but to build lasting relationships with our patients based on trust, transparency, and exceptional clinical outcomes. Serving the vibrant Rohtak community, we've earned the privilege of caring for thousands of families.

With a team led by highly qualified specialists and state-of-the-art facilities, Oriiface is committed to making quality dental care accessible, affordable, and anxiety-free for everyone who walks through our doors.

Dr. Vikramjet

BDS, MDS | Consultant Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon

With over 12 years of dedicated practice in oral and maxillofacial surgery, Dr. Vikramjet brings a wealth of clinical expertise to every procedure. His approach combines technical excellence with genuine compassion for patient comfort and outcomes. Whether performing complex surgical cases or straightforward restorative work, he applies the same meticulous attention and modern techniques to ensure the best results.

Dr. Vikramjet's philosophy centers on patient education and empowerment. He believes that informed patients make better decisions about their oral health and are more committed to long-term care. His warm demeanor and clear communication have earned him the trust of thousands of satisfied patients across Rohtak and surrounding regions.
