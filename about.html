Who We Are

Rohtak's Trusted Dental
Care Since Day One

Born out of a vision to bring specialist-grade dental care to Rohtak, Oriiface has grown into one of the most trusted names in dental health in Haryana.

Located at 19, Delhi Road near Maharshi Dayanand University, we built Oriiface Dental Care & Maxillofacial Center with a single goal: to treat every patient the way we'd want our own family treated. With compassion. With precision. With honesty.

Our clinic combines the latest dental technology with a warm, welcoming environment — because we believe going to the dentist shouldn't feel intimidating. Whether it's your child's first check-up or a complex implant procedure, we approach every case with the same level of care and expertise.

Over the years, we've built our reputation patient by patient — and with 5,055+ five-star Google reviews, our patients speak for us better than we ever could.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Everything we do at Oriiface is guided by these principles — from how we design our clinic to how we talk to our patients.

Patient First

Every decision we make — from treatment planning to scheduling — is made with your comfort and well-being as the top priority.

Clinical Excellence

We invest continuously in education, technology, and training so our patients receive care that meets international standards.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden costs, no surprises. We discuss treatment costs upfront so you can make informed decisions without pressure.

Personalized Care

No two smiles are the same. We create individual treatment plans tailored to your unique dental health and aesthetic goals.

Modern Technology

Digital X-rays, rotary endodontics, and advanced sterilization protocols — the latest tools for the safest, most efficient care.

Community Roots

We are proud members of the Rohtak community. Giving back through education, awareness camps, and accessible pricing is who we are.
